# Limitations

- The input data in this repo are synthetic and illustrative.
- PD and LGD values are demo-ready final layers, not calibrated production models.
- The pricing module is a hurdle framework, not a full economic-profit or capital model.
- Stress scenarios are deterministic overlays and do not model migration matrices, correlations, or Monte Carlo loss distributions.
- EAD treatment is simplified to funded balance plus CCF on undrawn revolver limits.
- The engine is designed to show methodology and system structure rather than claim regulatory readiness.
